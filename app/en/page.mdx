# Deshi Startup Guide

**Starting from SSC/HSC — build your own startup, from Bangladesh.**

This is an open knowledge base and practical playbook for Bangladeshi founders. Here, you will learn how to validate ideas, register businesses, find customers, raise funds, and scale from Bangladesh — all in plain English.

---

## Why this Guide?

Information about building a startup in Bangladesh is scattered across government websites, Facebook groups, blogs, and word of mouth. It is hard to know what is accurate, what is outdated, and where to look.

Our goal is:
- **All-in-one place** — from ideation to scaling.
- **Plain English** — simplified business terms.
- **Practical** — step-by-step guides, not just theory.
- **Verified** — cross-checked with official regulatory sources.
- **Accessible** — no business degree required.

---

## What You Will Learn

### Foundations
- What a startup is (and isn't)

### Registration & Legalities
- Company registration (RJSC)
- Trade licenses
- TIN, VAT, BIN setups
- Founders' agreements & legal roadmaps

### Operations
- Bank accounts, bKash, and payment systems
- Setting up your first website
- Sourcing and logistics
