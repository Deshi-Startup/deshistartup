# Step-by-step Roadmap – Idea to Scale

> **Summary:** If you cannot tell where to start, follow these four phases. Each phase says what to prove now, which paperwork applies and which topic sections to read. Guides are organized by topic; the phases show them in working order.

## The four phases

1. [Phase 1 – Idea, validation and the first formal footing](/en/roadmap/validate). Pick a problem, talk to customers, test demand with small experiments, and learn which paperwork you need now.
2. [Phase 2 – Product, team and rules](/en/roadmap/build). Stand the product up, sort co-founders and the first team, build accounting and compliance habits.
3. [Phase 3 – Selling, growth and funding](/en/roadmap/grow). Bring in customers on a schedule, manage delivery and COD, watch the metrics, raise funding if needed.
4. [Phase 4 – Scale, ecosystem and institution-building](/en/roadmap/scale). Use your sector playbook, tap government support, build an organization that lasts.

Not everyone moves in the same order – some sell first and sort the paperwork later. Treat the phases as a checklist, not a syllabus: start wherever your unanswered questions are.
