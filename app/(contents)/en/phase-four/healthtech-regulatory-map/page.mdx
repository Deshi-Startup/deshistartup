# Healthtech Regulatory Map

> **Overview:** Understanding the Healthtech Regulatory Map helps you better grasp your sector-specific growth path, regulators, customer behavior, and operational bottlenecks.

## Why is this topic important?

By properly understanding the Healthtech Regulatory Map, you can plan in advance how to scale your business, what support you can access, and what mistakes to avoid. This topic can be used as a guide as part of your Phase 4 planning.

## How is this relevant in Bangladesh?

In Bangladesh, the ecosystem, policy, community, and sector-specific knowledge for startups or SMEs work together. Therefore, this topic is not just information; it serves as an effective tool for an execution roadmap.

## What should you consider first?

- What is the impact of this topic on your business model?
- Which partners, regulators, or support organizations will be useful to you?
- Which steps should be taken first to get the most value?
- What mistakes might cost you more later?

## Step-by-step approach

1. Understand the real needs of the customer, buyer, distributor, and regulator in your sector.
2. Create a small validation plan—where will the leads come from, how will payment happen, and how will delivery happen.
3. Give importance to privacy, prescription, licensing, and trust signals in healthtech.
4. Incorporate logistics, compliance, supplier reliability, and customer service into your approach.
5. Launch a pilot and track metrics—conversion, refund, repeat rate, margin.
6. Look into utilizing any sector-specific subsidies or support.

## Common Mistakes

- If the sector is not understood, the product will not reach the market correctly.
- It is not enough to just say, "that sector is big"; checking execution feasibility is essential.
- Ignoring regulatory and operational complexity will cause major problems later.
