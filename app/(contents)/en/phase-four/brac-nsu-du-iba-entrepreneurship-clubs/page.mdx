> **Overview:** By understanding the BRAC, NSU, DU, IBA entrepreneurship clubs, you will gain a better understanding of your sector-specific growth path, regulators, customer behavior, and operational bottlenecks.

## Step-by-Step Action

1. Understand the real needs of the customer, buyer, distributor, and regulator in your sector.
2. Create a small validation plan–where will the leads come from, how will payment happen, and how will delivery happen.
3. Incorporate logistics, compliance, supplier reliability, and customer service into your approach.
4. Conduct a pilot launch and track metrics–conversion, refund, repeat rate, margin.
5. Look into utilizing any sector-specific subsidies or support.

- Ignoring regulatory and operational complexity will cause big problems later.
