# Product & Technology – Building for Bangladesh Realities

> **Summary:** Most users in Bangladesh are on phones, many on low-end Android, and connectivity is uneven. Product decisions must account for Bangla UX, light apps, SMS costs and data protection. This section holds the guides for those decisions.

If you can start without code, see the [no-code MVP guide](/en/product/no-code-mvp). And finishing [idea validation](/en/validation) before building saves both time and money.
