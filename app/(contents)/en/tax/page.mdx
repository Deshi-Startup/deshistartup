# Tax, VAT & Bookkeeping – From e-TIN to Returns

> **Summary:** Tax work is about sequence, not fear. First e-TIN, then VAT/BIN if needed, then the habit of monthly and year-end returns. This section breaks each step into its own guide, along with how to start keeping books.

To see what applies when, read [e-TIN, VAT & BIN](/en/e-tin-vat-bin) first. If you are unsure whether VAT registration applies, the [turnover tax and VAT thresholds](/en/tax/vat-thresholds) guide will help.
