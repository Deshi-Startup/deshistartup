# Ideas & Market Research – Picking a Problem and a Market

> **Summary:** Good startups come less from good ideas and more from real problems. This section collects the guides on finding problems, sizing a market, locating data sources and mapping competitors.

A good place to start is the [guide to finding ideas](/en/ideas/finding-ideas). Once an idea takes shape, go to [Idea Validation](/en/validation) and test it against real customers before building anything.
