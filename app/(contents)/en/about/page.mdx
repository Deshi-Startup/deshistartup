# About Deshi Startup and Our Editorial Policy

> **In short:** Deshi Startup is a free, open-source knowledge base for building a startup in Bangladesh. Bangla is the source edition and English is its companion. Contributors write and maintain the guides, with official sources prioritized for laws, taxes, fees and rules. This is not a government website or a professional advisory service; confirm current official requirements before making a consequential decision.

## What we are building

Building a new and scalable business in Bangladesh raises many separate questions. Deshi Startup brings the practical work into one place, from idea validation through registration, tax, payments, customers, teams and funding. The guides are written for Bangladesh-specific realities such as Facebook-first sales, cash on delivery, mobile financial services such as bKash and Nagad, and trust-building in a low-trust market.

Deshi Startup is not a government agency, legal or tax adviser, investment firm or paid course. Reading a guide requires neither payment nor an account.

## Who writes and maintains the guides

Deshi Startup is not a single-author publication. Founders, students, professionals and subject-matter contributors write guides, identify errors and add sources. Pages therefore identify **Deshi Startup contributors** as the maintainer rather than attributing the work to a fictional individual.

The [public GitHub history](https://github.com/Deshi-Startup/deshistartup) records when a page changed, what changed and who proposed it. The **Edit**, **History** and **Report a mistake** links on every guide point into that same public record.

## How we choose information and sources

The more a claim can change a founder's decision, the stronger its evidence needs to be. We seek sources in this order:

1. legislation, official gazettes, regulators and government portals;
2. recognized research institutions, original reports or datasets;
3. reliable news and specialist publications; and
4. a company's own material, clearly labelled as a company claim.

Fees, thresholds, forms and rules are dated or carry a verification date. Important claims place their source close to the relevant text. When reliable information is unavailable, an assumption is not presented as fact. The full rules for research, teaching and examples are in our [editorial guide](https://github.com/Deshi-Startup/deshistartup/blob/main/EDITORIAL.md); the [Bangla style guide](https://github.com/Deshi-Startup/deshistartup/blob/main/STYLE.md) governs the source edition's language.

## Publication, updates and corrections

Planned topics carry a **to be written** notice. Until a page is a complete guide, it is excluded from search indexing and from the published sitemap. Before complete content is released, its facts, sources, language, links and potential for harm are reviewed.

A normal last-updated date is different from the date on which a legal or regulatory claim was reverified. Relevant pages show those dates separately. If you find a problem, use the **Report a mistake** link on that page. For an urgent correction, [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/Deshi-Startup/deshistartup/issues/new).

## Limits and responsibility

This site helps you understand a decision; it does not make the decision for you. Government processes can vary by location, business type and date. Before filing, confirm the current form, fee and rule with the relevant government portal or office. For consequential decisions involving contracts, ownership, tax, foreign exchange or substantial financial risk, use a lawyer, chartered accountant or other appropriately licensed professional.
