# Licenses & Approvals – Which Business Needs Which Paper

> **Summary:** Not every business needs every license. Almost everyone needs a trade license; fire, environment, BSTI, BFSA or DGDA depend on what you do. This section breaks the approvals into separate guides so you only spend time on the ones that apply.

If you are brand new, read the [trade license guide](/en/trade-license) first. Company paperwork lives in [Business Registration](/en/registration), and tax/VAT work in the [Tax & VAT](/en/tax) section.
