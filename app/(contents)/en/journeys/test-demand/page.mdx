# I want to test demand – from landing page to pre-orders

> **In short:** "Will people buy?" is answered by small tests, not by building the product. This path takes you from an assumption through a landing page, interviews, and pre-orders to a clear go/no-go decision.

This path is for founders whose idea is fairly clear but who want real evidence of demand before spending serious time or money. In Bangladesh, acting on "nice idea" praise is risky; you need signals of money or a firm commitment.

## The path, step by step

1. **Write the assumption and plan the demand test.** Decide what evidence counts as a "yes." → [Validate demand without building](/en/phase-one/how-to-validate-demand-without-building)
2. **Prepare your interview script.** Use Bangla and English questions that surface real behavior. → [Customer interview scripts](/en/phase-one/customer-interview-scripts-in-bangla-and-english)
3. **Build a simple landing page.** Include the offer, price, and a way to capture interest. → [Landing page template](/en/phase-three/landing-page-template)
4. **Test demand in Facebook groups.** Gauge response where your potential customers already are. → [Testing demand in Facebook groups](/en/phase-one/testing-demand-in-facebook-groups)
5. **Run a small-budget Facebook ad test.** Read interest from click and sign-up rates. → [Facebook ad test planner](/en/phase-three/facebook-ad-test-planner)
6. **Choose pre-orders vs a waitlist.** Separate strong signals (advance payment) from weak ones (just a name). → [Pre-orders vs waitlists](/en/phase-one/pre-orders-vs-waitlists-in-bangladesh)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Defined "success" as a number (e.g. 10 pre-orders)
- [ ] Published a landing page or post
- [ ] Talked to at least 10 people and kept notes
- [ ] Tried to collect advance payment or a written commitment
- [ ] Made a continue / pivot / stop decision from the results
