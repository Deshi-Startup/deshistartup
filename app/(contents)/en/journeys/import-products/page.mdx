# I want to import products

> **TL;DR:** Documents differ by product and shipment. Before ordering or sending money, check whether IRC, HS code, LC/LCAF, sector permits and duty or VAT apply. A wrong classification or incomplete document can add time and cost at the port.

This path is for anyone bringing products into Bangladesh for sale or use. Before choosing the product, confirm the import policy, customs tariff, relevant regulator, authorised dealer bank and process with an experienced customs clearing and forwarding agent.

## The path, step by step

1. **Understand import/export registration basics.** Learn what registrations you need first. → [Import/export registration basics](../phase-one/importexport-registration-basics)
2. **Match the IRC/ERC checklist.** Verify what documents the IRC requires. → [IRC/ERC checklist](../phase-one/ircerc-checklist)
3. **Get the IRC/ERC step by step.** Follow the application process. → [Step-by-step IRC/ERC guide](../phase-one/step-by-step-guide-to-ircerc)
4. **Find the right HS code.** Duties and rules depend on the HS code. → [Finding the right HS code](../phase-one/how-to-find-the-right-hs-code)
5. **Prepare the LC and bank documents.** Understand LC/LCAF and documents to pay the supplier. → [LC, LCAF & bank documentation basics](../phase-one/lc-lcaf-and-bank-documentation-basics)
6. **Choose a freight forwarder.** Compare and pick air, sea, or courier. → [Choosing a freight forwarder](../phase-one/choosing-a-freight-forwarder-air-sea-and-courier)
7. **Calculate landed cost.** Work out the true cost with duty, VAT, freight, and clearing. → [Landed cost calculator](../phase-one/landed-cost-calculator-duty-vat-freight-and-clearing)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Fixed the product category and supplier
- [ ] Got (or applied for) an IRC
- [ ] Found the correct HS code
- [ ] Prepared the LC and bank documents
- [ ] Calculated landed cost and set the price
