# I want to raise angel or VC funding

> **TL;DR:** Investors differ by stage, sector and evidence required. First decide whether equity funding fits the business. Then organise the metrics, pitch, records and a relevant investor list.

This path is for founders seeking angel or venture capital in exchange for equity. If the business is unlikely to reach a large market quickly, compare revenue, grants and the cost of debt as well.

## The path, step by step

1. **Organize your metrics.** Fix the numbers investors want to see. → [Startup metrics 101](/en/metrics/metrics-101)
2. **Build the pitch deck.** Lay out problem, solution, market, and traction clearly. → [Pitch deck template](/en/funding/pitch-deck)
3. **Set up an investor CRM.** Track who you spoke to and the next step. → [Investor CRM](/en/funding/investor-crm)
4. **Prepare a data room.** Organize the due-diligence documents in advance. → [Fundraising data room checklist](/en/funding/data-room)
5. **Reach the right investors.** Know the angels and networks and do outreach. → [Angel investors](/en/funding/angel-investors) · [Bangladesh Angels Network](/en/funding/bangladesh-angels)
6. **Understand the term sheet.** Grasp valuation and terms in plain language. → [Term sheet basics](/en/funding/term-sheets)
7. **Browse the investor directory.** Use the source and last-verification date on each entry. → [Investor directory](/en/directory/investors)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Organized core metrics and traction
- [ ] Built the pitch deck
- [ ] Built an investor list and CRM
- [ ] Prepared the data room
- [ ] Understood the key term-sheet terms
