# I want to raise angel or VC funding

> **In short:** Bangladesh has relatively few investors, and they want traction and clear numbers. This path prepares you from organizing your metrics through the pitch deck, outreach, and the term sheet.

This path is for founders who want to raise investment from angels or venture capital (VC) in exchange for equity. Before starting, make sure your business is genuinely VC-suitable (potential to grow fast); otherwise a loan or bootstrapping may be better.

## The path, step by step

1. **Organize your metrics.** Fix the numbers investors want to see. → [Startup metrics 101](/en/phase-three/startup-metrics-101)
2. **Build the pitch deck.** Lay out problem, solution, market, and traction clearly. → [Pitch deck template](/en/phase-three/pitch-deck-template)
3. **Set up an investor CRM.** Track who you spoke to and the next step. → [Investor CRM](/en/phase-three/investor-crm)
4. **Prepare a data room.** Organize the due-diligence documents in advance. → [Fundraising data room checklist](/en/phase-three/fundraising-data-room-checklist)
5. **Reach the right investors.** Know the angels and networks and do outreach. → [Angel investors](/en/phase-three/angel-investors) · [Bangladesh Angels Network](/en/phase-three/bangladesh-angels-network)
6. **Understand the term sheet.** Grasp valuation and terms in plain language. → [Term sheet basics](/en/phase-three/term-sheet-basics-in-plain-bangla)
7. **Browse the investor directory.** Use the list of verified investors. → [Investor directory](/en/directory/investors)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Organized core metrics and traction
- [ ] Built the pitch deck
- [ ] Built an investor list and CRM
- [ ] Prepared the data room
- [ ] Understood the key term-sheet terms
