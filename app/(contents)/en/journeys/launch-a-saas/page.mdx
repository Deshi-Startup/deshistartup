# I want to launch a SaaS from Bangladesh

> **In short:** Building SaaS from Bangladesh is possible, but you must understand up front how to sell to foreign customers and bring the money home. This path runs from market selection through company setup, foreign exchange, international payments, and customer acquisition.

This path is for diaspora and export-oriented founders selling software services to foreign markets. Alongside the product, understand contracts, invoices, receipt of foreign currency, bank records and tax treatment.

## The path, step by step

1. **Understand the reality of export SaaS.** Decide the market and the problem up front. → [Export-oriented SaaS from Bangladesh](../phase-one/export-oriented-saas-from-bangladesh)
2. **Read the SaaS export playbook.** Get the full picture of sales, billing, and operations. → [SaaS export playbook](../phase-four/saas-export-playbook-from-bangladesh)
3. **Choose the structure.** Check which entity and records the foreign customer, bank and payment provider require, then time registration accordingly. → [Business registration](../registration)
4. **Learn the foreign-exchange rules.** Understand remittance and foreign-shareholder rules. → [Foreign exchange basics for SaaS export](../phase-one/foreign-exchange-basics-for-saas-export-remittance-and-foreign-shareholders)
5. **Understand international payment limits.** Know the real constraints on cards and payment links. → [International payment & card constraints](../phase-one/international-payment-card-and-payment-link-constraints)
6. **Track SaaS metrics.** Measure MRR, churn, and payback from day one. → [SaaS metrics](../phase-three/saas-metrics)
7. **Verify export incentives.** Confirm current IT/ITES eligibility and circulars on official sources. → [IT/ITES export incentives](../phase-four/export-incentives-for-itites)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Fixed the target market and customer
- [ ] Set up the company structure and registration
- [ ] Understood remittance/foreign-exchange rules
- [ ] Decided how to accept international payments
- [ ] Started tracking core SaaS metrics
