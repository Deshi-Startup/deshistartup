# I want to register a private limited company

> **In short:** Opening a private limited company is more than filing a name at RJSC – structure choice, name clearance, documents, registration, tax, banking, and annual filing all happen in one sequence. This path puts each step in the right order.

This path is for founders who want to formally register a private limited company (Ltd.) – usually because of funding, corporate clients, or multiple shareholders. Government rules can change at each step, so confirm major decisions with a chartered accountant or lawyer.

## The path, step by step

1. **Choose the company type.** Compare sole proprietorship, partnership, and private limited. → [Company types](/en/company-types)
2. **Compare the three structures.** Understand the cost and liability differences before deciding. → [Sole proprietorship vs partnership vs private limited](/en/phase-one/sole-proprietorship-vs-partnership-vs-private-limited-company)
3. **Get RJSC name clearance.** Check that your preferred name is conflict-free and clear it. → [RJSC & name clearance](/en/rjsc-name-clearance)
4. **Prepare the MoA and AoA.** Set the company's objectives and governing documents. → [MoA & AoA basics](/en/phase-one/memorandum-and-articles-of-association-basics)
5. **Complete registration at RJSC.** File the registration step by step and pay the fees. → [Private limited registration step by step](/en/phase-one/private-limited-company-registration-step-by-step)
6. **Prepare for tax and VAT.** Get the company e-TIN and BIN if required. → [e-TIN & VAT/BIN](/en/e-tin-vat-bin)
7. **Open a business bank account.** Open an account in the company's name with the paperwork ready. → [Business bank account](/en/phase-one/business-bank-account-guide)
8. **Keep annual compliance in order.** Set up the returns, AGM, and filing calendar. → [Annual compliance checklist](/en/phase-one/annual-compliance-checklist)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Finalized the structure (Ltd.) decision
- [ ] Got name clearance
- [ ] Prepared MoA/AoA
- [ ] Completed RJSC registration
- [ ] Opened e-TIN and a bank account
- [ ] Put annual filing dates on the calendar
