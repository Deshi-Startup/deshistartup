# I want to start an online business – license to launch

> **In short:** An online business is more than a Facebook page – you have to set up payments, courier, returns, bookkeeping, and the minimum legal paperwork together. This path takes you from choosing a business type through licensing, payments, and delivery to launch.

This path is for anyone selling a product or service through a Facebook page, WhatsApp or a website. Depending on the customer, payment may be an advance, MFS, bank, gateway or cash on delivery. Plan payment, delivery, refunds and documents together.

## The path, step by step

1. **Decide your business type.** Startup, SME, or ordinary commerce – settle this first. → [Startup, SME, agency or e-commerce](../start-here/startup-vs-sme-vs-agency-vs-e-commerce-business-in-bangladesh)
2. **Check the trade-license rules.** Verify the local process for the business address and category. → [Trade license](../trade-license)
3. **Understand e-TIN and VAT/BIN.** Handle the minimum tax and VAT prep. → [e-TIN & VAT/BIN](../e-tin-vat-bin)
4. **Set up payments.** Choose among bKash/Nagad, cash-on-delivery, and a gateway. → [Payments](../payments)
5. **Set up courier and delivery.** Compare service area, charges, returns, compensation and complaint handling. → [Courier/logistics options](../phase-three/courierlogistics-options)
6. **Organize Facebook commerce and launch.** Arrange the page, inbox, order, and support flow. → [Facebook commerce playbook](../phase-three/facebook-commerce-playbook)
7. **Comply with the Digital Commerce policy.** Learn the mandatory rules for an online shop. → [Digital Commerce policy](../phase-three/digital-commerce-policy-and-what-it-requires-of-an-online-shop)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Fixed the business type and first product
- [ ] Got (or started) a trade license and e-TIN
- [ ] Chose payments (wallet/COD/gateway)
- [ ] Picked a courier provider and wrote a return policy
- [ ] Wrote out the refund, support, and order flow
