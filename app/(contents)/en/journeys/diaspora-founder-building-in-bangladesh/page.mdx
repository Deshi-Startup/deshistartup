# I am a diaspora founder building in Bangladesh

> **TL;DR:** A diaspora founder may benefit from an international network or experience, while distance can also hide market and operating problems. Write who decides locally, who controls money and records, and which foreign-investment rules apply.

This path is for non-resident Bangladeshi founders building or investing in Bangladesh. With a local operator or co-founder, put roles, bank authority, information access, spending limits and disputes in writing. Confirm foreign-investment and foreign-exchange rules with an authorised dealer bank and qualified adviser.

## The path, step by step

1. **Understand diaspora-founder constraints.** Recognize the real challenges of distance and rules. → [Diaspora founder constraints](../founder-life/diaspora-founder-constraints)
2. **Use your advantages.** Learn how to leverage network, capital, and experience. → [Diaspora founder advantages](../founder-life/diaspora-founder-advantages)
3. **Set up a local operator/co-founder.** A trustworthy partner on the ground is the foundation. → [Diaspora co-founder & local operator relationship](../founder-life/diaspora-co-founder-and-local-operator-relationship)
4. **Understand foreign-owned company setup.** Learn the rules for opening a foreign-owned company. → [Foreign-owned company basics](../phase-one/basics-of-starting-a-foreign-owned-company-in-bangladesh)
5. **Comply with FDI rules.** Understand foreign investment and diaspora-funding rules. → [FDI rules & diaspora funding](../phase-three/foreign-investmentfdi-rules-and-diaspora-funding)
6. **Set up banking and foreign exchange.** Learn the rules for moving money and shareholding. → [Foreign exchange basics](../phase-one/foreign-exchange-basics-for-saas-export-remittance-and-foreign-shareholders)
7. **Use the diaspora-funding path.** Explore NRB/diaspora investment opportunities. → [NRB/diaspora funding path](../phase-three/nrb-or-diaspora-funding-path)
