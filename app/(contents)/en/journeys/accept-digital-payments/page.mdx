# I want to accept digital payments

> **In short:** Accepting payments in Bangladesh is more than dropping in a gateway — bank account, wallets, merchant account, settlement, and refunds all have to work together. This path sets up the whole payment system, from business type onward.

This path is for founders who want to collect money from customers online or by mobile. Card usage is still low; most transactions run on bKash/Nagad and cash-on-delivery — so reconciliation matters from the start.

## The path, step by step

1. **Get the overall picture of payments.** Decide which channel money comes through and how. → [Payments](/en/payments)
2. **Open a business bank account.** Prepare where settlement money will land. → [Business bank account](/en/phase-one/business-bank-account-guide)
3. **Choose wallets and a gateway.** Compare the basics of bKash/Nagad and payment gateways. → [bKash/Nagad & payment gateway basics](/en/phase-one/bkashnagadpayment-gateway-basics)
4. **Get a merchant account.** Learn what's needed to open a merchant account. → [Merchant account documents](/en/phase-one/what-you-need-to-open-a-merchant-account)
5. **Integrate the gateway.** Pick among SSLCommerz/aamarPay/PortWallet and integrate. → [Payment gateway integration](/en/phase-one/payment-gateway-integration-sslcommerz-amarpay-portwallet)
6. **Set up reconciliation.** Reconcile gateway, wallet, bank, and COD figures. → [Payment reconciliation](/en/phase-one/payment-reconciliation-across-gateway-wallet-bank-and-cod)
7. **Handle refunds and disputes.** Set rules for refunds, chargebacks, and disputes. → [Refunds, chargebacks & disputes](/en/phase-one/refunds-chargebacks-and-dispute-handling)

## Checklist for this path

- [ ] Decided which channels to accept money through
- [ ] Opened a business bank account
- [ ] Got a merchant/gateway account
- [ ] Wrote the settlement and reconciliation rules
- [ ] Set the refund and dispute policy
