# Intellectual Property & Trademarks – Protecting Your Brand and Work

> **Summary:** Your brand name, logo, software and content are often a startup's real assets. Trademark, copyright and patent are three different protections, and not everyone needs all of them. The guides in this section show which one matters in your case.

Before finalizing a name, run the [trademark conflict check](/en/ip/trademark-conflict-check) – renaming later is expensive.
