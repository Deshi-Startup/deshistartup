# Startup vs SME: Which One Are You Building?

> **In short:** Not every business is a startup, and not every startup is an SME. The answer becomes clear when you understand your goal, growth model, and funding strategy.

## What Is the Difference?

| Topic | Startup | SME |
|------|---------|-----|
| Goal | Scale quickly with a new product or service | Operate steadily and profitably |
| Growth | Fast growth, often with industry-level ambition | Local or nearby market growth |
| Funding | Investors, angels, VC | Personal capital, loans |
| Risk | Higher risk and uncertainty | Lower risk and more predictable |
| Product | Innovation or technology-driven | Existing product or service |
| Exit | IPO or acquisition may be possible | Usually a family-run or owner-operated business |

## When You Are a Startup

You are likely building a startup when:

- You are trying to create a new product or service.
- Your goal is national or global scale.
- You want to build a growth-driven team.
- You are interested in external investment.
- You are working through uncertain market validation.

## When You Are an SME

An SME is usually a better fit when:

- The goal is steady profit.
- The market is small or medium-sized.
- You want a lower-risk path.
- You plan to grow with personal savings or bank loans.
- You are running a repeatable business model.

## Startup or SME in Bangladesh: Which Should You Choose?

### Questions for Choosing a Startup

- Is your product new and scalable?
- Do you want fast growth?
- Are you trying to raise investment?
- Can you take significant risk?
- Do you have a technical or product development team?

### Questions for Choosing an SME

- Do you want simplicity?
- Do you want steady cash flow?
- Do you want to run a local business?
- Do you want to keep the team size limited?
- Do you want a long-term family-run business?

## Examples

### Startup

- A mobile app that helps rural shopkeepers place wholesale orders.
- A SaaS platform that provides automation for restaurants.
- A sharing-economy service that lets people book seats on buses in Bangladesh.

### SME

- A local restaurant or cafe.
- A bachelor housing real estate agency.
- A small uniform printing service.

## Which One Is Right?

The right answer depends on your goals and strengths.

- If you want innovation, speed, and investment-led growth, choose a startup.
- If you want a safer, more predictable, local business, choose an SME.

> Remember: no startup becomes a real startup on day one. Validate carefully first.
