# Finding Customers: First Sales and Trust in Bangladesh

> **TL;DR:** Finding customers is not the same as running ads. In product-based online businesses, the page, inbox, phone, payment, delivery and refund process work together. Choose a specific first customer, then run small tests to learn which message, channel and offer earns a response.

Do not wait until the product is finished to ask where customers will come from. Early customers may come through Facebook, Messenger, WhatsApp, an existing network, a community, referrals, a market visit or direct office outreach. The mix depends on the business.

## Define the First Customer

“Everyone” is not a customer segment. A specific first group makes channel, language, offer, price and support decisions easier.

Weak: “We help online sellers.”

Stronger: “We help clothing sellers outside Dhaka reconcile cash-on-delivery returns.”

Ask:

- Where do they spend time?
- Whom do they trust?
- How do they handle the problem now?
- Which words do they use when complaining?
- What do they need to know before asking the price?
- Who else influences the decision?

## Starting with Facebook

Many small Bangladesh businesses begin with a Facebook page. Even without a website, the page should quickly explain what is sold, the location, how to order and whom to contact if something goes wrong.

Set up:

- a clear name and real product or work images
- a plain description of what you sell and for whom
- phone, Messenger, WhatsApp or address where appropriate
- delivery area, payment methods and return or refund rules
- genuine reviews shown with permission
- useful posts, not only offers

In groups, read the rules before posting. Answer relevant questions and mention the product only when it fits the discussion. Repeated link dumping damages trust and may violate group rules.

## Treat the Inbox as a Sales Counter

Many small online sales are discussed in Messenger or WhatsApp. Make the reply clear and complete. If you cannot answer quickly, say when you will return.

```text
Thanks. Which product, size and location do you need?
We deliver to [area]. Payment options: [methods].
To confirm, we need the name, phone number and address.
```

Before confirming, make the product, price, delivery timing, payment and problem-resolution terms clear. Record the confirmed details in writing.

## Clarify the Offer Before Advertising

An ad does not fix a vague offer. Explain which problem is reduced, what proof exists, what risk is removed and how to order. Test small variations before increasing spend.

| Test | What it may teach |
|---|---|
| Different product images | Which presentation stops attention |
| Different messages | Whether people respond to the problem, price or proof |
| Message button vs website | Where customers prefer to continue |
| Dhaka vs another area | How response and fulfilment differ by location |
| Cash on delivery vs advance | How payment choice affects returns and trust |

The first campaign is for learning. Record repeated questions, objections, drop-off points and delivery outcomes.

## Decide Whether a Website Helps

A small Facebook test can run with a page, inbox, phone and sheet. A website becomes more useful when the catalogue grows, search traffic matters, a gateway is needed, customers must sign up or a corporate buyer wants to verify the company and offer.

| Situation | Website decision |
|---|---|
| Small Facebook test | Often not needed initially |
| Building a standalone brand | Add one as the case becomes clear |
| Software or digital product | Usually useful for explanation, demo or sign-up |
| Corporate customers | Makes verification easier |
| Search acquisition | Needed for pages that can be indexed |

A website does not remove the need for responsive support.

## Build Trust Through Behaviour

A new business may face questions about product authenticity, delivery, refunds and whether a real person will answer. Address those questions directly:

- show the actual product with your own images and videos
- do not present stock images as the real item
- publish price, delivery charge and return terms
- provide a working contact channel
- show genuine reviews only with permission
- send confirmation and tracking updates
- answer problems instead of going silent

Saying “we are trustworthy” is weaker than behaving consistently.

## Selling Outside Dhaka

There is opportunity outside Dhaka, but the same fulfilment plan may not transfer unchanged. Delivery time and cost, preferred payment method, local language, familiarity and cash-on-delivery risk vary by location.

Test one area at a time. Track delivery time, return cost, successful collection and repeat purchase before scaling ads.

## Retain What You Learn

Follow up after delivery. Ask whether the product arrived correctly and what should improve. Request a review from a satisfied customer; resolve an unhappy customer's issue promptly.

For the first customers, a sheet is enough:

| Field | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Source | Shows which channel works |
| Questions | Improves the page and copy |
| Bought or declined | Tests offer and price |
| Payment method | Shows cash-flow and risk |
| Delivery outcome | Reveals courier and return problems |
| Repeat purchase | Signals product and trust quality |
