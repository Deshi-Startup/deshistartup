# Phase 1 Roadmap

This page will clarify your first startup journey for Phase 1.

## Objective of Phase 1

- To create a simple, Bangla-based practical guide for starting a startup in Bangladesh.
- To cover 5 key areas: Idea validation, business registration, trade license, payment systems, and customer acquisition.
- To build an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) that provides clear instructions for new founders.

## Sequence of Your First Tasks

### 1. Idea Validation
- **Validate the Problem:** Is the problem you are trying to solve genuinely present in people's lives?
- **Talk to Customers:** Speak with at least 10-20 potential customers to gather their feedback.
- **Test with a Small MVP:** Create a prototype or landing page at low cost and gauge the response.
- [Read more](../idea-validation)

### 2. Funding & Capital
- **Bootstrapping:** Collect initial capital from personal savings or family/friends.
- **Cost Reduction:** Avoid unnecessary expenses (like expensive offices) in the beginning and focus on product development.

### 3. Registration & Legal
- **Select the Right Entity:** Understand whether Sole Proprietorship, Partnership, or Private Limited Company is right for you.
- **Company Registration with RJSC:** If it's a limited company, know the steps required by RJSC.
- **TIN/VAT/BIN:** Be aware of initial tax and VAT documents alongside the Trade License.
- **Founder's Agreement:** If there are multiple founders, create a written agreement (or at least a clear understanding) to divide shares and responsibilities from the start.
- [Read more](../registration)

### 4. Trade License
- **Local City Corporation/Municipality:** Obtain the Trade License based on your business address.
- **Application Documents:** Keep documents like the rental agreement, NID, photos, etc., ready.
- [Read more](../trade-license)

### 5. Payments
- **Payment Ecosystem:** Understand the use of bKash, Nagad, COD (Cash on Delivery), and Bank payments in Bangladesh.
- **Right Payment Mix:** Determine the payment method based on your business type (e.g., COD is common in e-commerce, while cards/bKash are common in SaaS).
- [Read more](../payments)

### 6. Marketing & Customer Acquisition
- **Zero-budget Marketing:** Use organic social media (Facebook Group, LinkedIn) and Word-of-mouth.
- **Facebook-first Growth:** Create a page and reach a targeted audience.
- **Sales Funnel:** Develop a process to close sales by talking directly through Messenger or WhatsApp.
- **Trust Building:** Build trust through customer reviews and accurate delivery.
- [Read more](../customers)

## Phase 1 Starting Checklist

- [ ] Problem validation completed and a small MVP created.
- [ ] Initial capital (Bootstrapping) secured.
- [ ] Business name finalized, and initial legal documents (TIN, Founder Agreement) and Trade License prepared.
- [ ] Bank/bKash/Nagad account or COD payment setup for receiving payments.
- [ ] First 10 paid customers acquired.
