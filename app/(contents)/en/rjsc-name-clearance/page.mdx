# RJSC Name Clearance and Company Registration: Where to Go

> **TL;DR:** Treat an RJSC private limited company application as two steps. First obtain name clearance. Once the name is approved, apply for registration with the MoA, AoA, prescribed forms and government fees. Rather than duplicate instructions here, the two guides below maintain the current steps, documents and costs separately.

## 1. Obtain Name Clearance

Name clearance is RJSC's preliminary permission to use a proposed name. It is not a Certificate of Incorporation. According to RJSC information checked on 10 July 2026, the fee is BDT 500 for each proposed name and clearance is generally valid for 30 days.

For name search, application, payment, validity and common mistakes:

**[RJSC Name Clearance: Step by Step](phase-one/rjsc-name-clearance)**

## 2. Apply for Company Registration

After the name is approved, you submit information about the company's objects, shares, directors, shareholders and registered office. Prescribed documents include the MoA, AoA and forms such as “Form I”, “Form VI”, “Form IX”, “Form X” and “Form XII”. Government fees depend on authorised share capital and the documents filed.

For the document list, fee example, application and post-incorporation tasks:
