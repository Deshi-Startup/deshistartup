# Directory – Verified Bangladesh Startup Ecosystem List

> **TL;DR:** Inclusion is not an endorsement or a promise of funding. Check the public source, stage, sector and last-verification date on each entry. If cheque size, deadline or equity terms are not stated, do not infer them. Re-check the organisation's own site on the day you apply.

## How the Directory Is Maintained

Entries come from `data/directory/*.json`. A new organisation is checked against an official site or reliable public profile. If cheque size, deadline or equity terms are not public, the field remains `Not publicly stated`. The target cadence is quarterly re-verification.
