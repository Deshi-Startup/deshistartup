# Co-founders, Equity & Founder Agreements

> **Summary:** In Bangladesh, many startups break on co-founder disputes before the product does. Who does what, how shares split, what happens if someone leaves – writing this down early is cheap; settling it later is expensive. The guides in this section help with that writing.

Start with [solo vs team](/en/cofounders/solo-vs-team), then read [founder agreement basics](/en/cofounders/founder-agreement) and [equity splits and vesting](/en/cofounders/equity-and-vesting).
