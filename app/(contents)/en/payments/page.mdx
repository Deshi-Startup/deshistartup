# Payment Systems

> **In short:** In Bangladesh, payments are not only about card swipes. bKash, Nagad, COD (Cash on Delivery), bank transfers, and gateways all create a unique payment ecosystem. Choosing the right payment method for your startup can directly affect customer acquisition.

---

## Bangladesh’s Payment Landscape

Bangladesh’s payment system is different from much of the world. A few useful signals:

| Data point | Number |
|------------|--------|
| Mobile penetration | Around 180 million+ |
| Internet users | Around 130 million |
| Bank accounts | Around 100 million |
| Debit/credit cards | Around 40 million, still relatively low |
| MFS accounts such as bKash, Nagad | Around 100 million+ |
| Card penetration | Around 4%, very low |

> **Key point:** In Bangladesh, **MFS (Mobile Financial Services)** is much more popular than cards. Your payment strategy should almost always include bKash/Nagad.

## Payment Methods

### 1. Mobile Financial Services (MFS)

#### bKash

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Market share** | Largest player, around 60%+ of the MFS market |
| **Users** | 60 million+ |
| **Use cases** | Send Money, Payment, Cash Out |
| **Merchant account** | bKash Merchant Account |
| **Charge** | Personal: free within certain limits; merchant: around 1.5%-2.5% |
| **API/Payment Gateway** | Available for developers |
| **Website** | [bkash.com](https://www.bkash.com) |

**How to open a merchant account:**

1. Go to the “Merchant” section on the bKash website.
2. Fill out the application form.
3. Submit required documents such as trade license, TIN, NID, and company documents.
4. After verification, the merchant account will be activated.
5. You can start collecting payments.

**bKash Payment Gateway for apps/websites:**

- bKash has its own payment gateway.
- You can integrate bKash payment into your website or app.
- Customers can pay through the bKash app or USSD (*247#).

#### Nagad

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Market share** | Second largest |
| **Users** | 50 million+ |
| **Use cases** | Send Money, Payment, Cash Out |
| **Merchant account** | Nagad Merchant |
| **Charge** | Similar to bKash |
| **Website** | [nagad.com.bd](https://nagad.com.bd) |

#### Other MFS Providers

| Service | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| Rocket (DBBL) | Dutch-Bangla Bank’s MFS |
| Upay | City Bank’s MFS |
| Tap | QR-based payment |

### 2. Bank Transfer

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **BEFTN** | Electronic fund transfer, batch processing, 1-2 days |
| **NPSB** | Instant interbank transfer |
| **RTGS** | For large amounts, instant, minimum Tk 100,000 |
| **Mobile Banking App** | Each bank has its own app |

**When to use it:**

- B2B payments, because most businesses prefer bank transfers
- Large transactions
- Salary payments

### 3. Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD is the most popular payment method for e-commerce in Bangladesh.

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What it is** | Customer pays after receiving the product |
| **Popularity** | Around 60-70% of e-commerce orders |
| **Problems** | High return rate, fake orders, cash handling risk |
| **Best for** | Physical product businesses |

**Tips to reduce COD risk:**

- [ ] Call to confirm the order.
- [ ] Send SMS/call before delivery.
- [ ] Take partial advance payment, even Tk 50-100.
- [ ] Check the customer’s order history.
- [ ] Set up a COD reconciliation process with your delivery partner.
- [ ] Filter fake addresses and phone numbers.

### 4. Payment Gateways (Online Payments)

If you want to accept online payments on your website or app:

#### SSLCommerz

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Largest gateway** | One of Bangladesh’s most popular payment gateways |
| **Supports** | bKash, Nagad, cards, bank transfer, Rocket, Upay, and more |
| **Charge** | Around 2.5%-3.5% per transaction |
| **Integration** | API, plugins for WooCommerce, Shopify |
| **Website** | [sslcommerz.com](https://sslcommerz.com) |

#### aamarPay

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Alternative** | Alternative to SSLCommerz |
| **Charge** | Around 2%-3% |
| **Integration** | API available |
| **Website** | [aamarpay.com](https://aamarpay.com) |

#### ShurjoPay

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Provider** | ShurjoMukhi Limited |
| **Charge** | Competitive |
| **Website** | [shurjopay.com](https://shurjopay.com) |

#### Payment Gateway Comparison

| Gateway | Transaction fee | Settlement time | Integration |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| SSLCommerz | Around 2.5%-3.5% | 2-3 days | Easy |
| aamarPay | Around 2%-3% | 2-3 days | Easy |

### 5. International Payments

If you receive money from foreign customers, such as SaaS exports or freelance services:

| Method | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **PayPal** | Not directly available in Bangladesh; workarounds are needed |
| **Wise (TransferWise)** | Can send money to a Bangladesh bank account |
| **Payoneer** | Popular with freelancers |
| **Bank wire transfer** | Direct bank transfer through SWIFT |
| **Stripe** | Not available in Bangladesh, but can be used through Atlas/LLC structures |

**For SaaS exports:**

- Send invoices to foreign clients from the Bangladeshi company.
- The client pays through bank wire or Wise.
- The payment arrives as foreign remittance through Bangladesh Bank channels.
- You may need to complete **Form C** through your bank.

## What Should Your Startup Use?

### E-commerce (Physical Product)

```text
✅ bKash Merchant
✅ Nagad Merchant
✅ Cash on Delivery (COD)
✅ Bank Transfer
✅ SSLCommerz/aamarPay (optional)
```

### SaaS / Digital Service

```text
✅ bKash Merchant
✅ SSLCommerz/aamarPay (credit/debit card)
✅ Bank Transfer (B2B)
✅ Wise/Payoneer (international customers)
```

### B2B Service

```text
✅ Bank Transfer (NPSB/BEFTN)
✅ bKash (small payments)
✅ Cheque (large companies)
```

### Marketplace / Platform

```text
✅ SSLCommerz/aamarPay (all methods)
✅ bKash + Nagad
✅ COD (if physical products)
```

## Payments and Trust

In Bangladesh, **trust** can matter even more than the payment method.

**Why people may avoid online payment:**

- Distrust of new brands
- Fear of losing money
- Previous scam experiences
- Preference for “see first, then buy”

**Ways to increase trust:**

- [ ] Professional website/landing page
- [ ] Clear return/refund policy
- [ ] Customer reviews/testimonials
- [ ] Multiple payment options, especially COD
- [ ] SSL certificate (https)
- [ ] Active social media presence
- [ ] Phone number/WhatsApp support
- [ ] Verified bKash/Nagad merchant account

## Bangladesh Bank Basics

### Opening a Business Bank Account

**What you need:**

- Certificate of Incorporation
- Trade License
- TIN Certificate
- Board Resolution for companies
- Directors’ NID and photos
- Required bank forms

**Popular business banking options:**

| Bank | Why it can be useful |
|------|----------------------|
| Dutch-Bangla Bank (DBBL) | Large network, strong digital banking |
| BRAC Bank | SME-friendly, good service |
| City Bank | Depositor-friendly |
| Eastern Bank (EBL) | Strong corporate banking |
| Islami Bank Bangladesh | For Islamic banking needs |
| Standard Chartered | International transactions |
| HSBC | Larger businesses and foreign transactions |
