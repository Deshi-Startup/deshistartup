# Trade License

> **In short:** A trade license is mandatory for operating any business in Bangladesh. You obtain it from your local city corporation or municipality. Operating without one is illegal and may lead to penalties.

---

## What Is a Trade License?

A trade license is an official permit proving that your business is allowed to operate under local government rules.

**Why you need it:**

- Mandatory for legally operating a business
- Needed to open a bank account
- Helps show credibility to suppliers and partners
- May be needed for VAT/BIN registration
- Often needed for shop or office agreements

---

## Where Do You Apply?

Based on your business location:

| Area | Where to apply |
|------|----------------|
| Dhaka North | [DNCC](https://dncc.gov.bd), Dhaka North City Corporation |
| Dhaka South | [DSCC](https://dscc.gov.bd), Dhaka South City Corporation |
| Chattogram | [CCC](https://ccc.gov.bd), Chattogram City Corporation |
| Sylhet | [SCC](https://scc.gov.bd), Sylhet City Corporation |
| Rajshahi | [Rajshahi CC](https://rajshahi.gov.bd), Rajshahi City Corporation |
| Khulna | [KCC](https://kcc.gov.bd), Khulna City Corporation |
| Other municipal areas | Relevant municipality |

---

## What Documents Are Needed?

### Required Documents

| Document | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Application form** | Form from the relevant city corporation or municipality |
| **National ID (NID)** | Copy of applicant’s NID |
| **Passport-size photos** | 1-2 copies |
| **TIN certificate** | Tax Identification Number |
| **Rental agreement** | For rented shop or office |
| **Layout/map** | Shop or office layout, required in some places |
| **Utility bill** | Gas, water, or electricity bill as address proof |
| **Previous trade license** | For renewal |

**Additional documents for companies:**

- Certificate of Incorporation
- MoA (Memorandum of Association)
- Board Resolution for the trade license

---

## Application Process: Step by Step

### Example: Dhaka City Corporation

**Offline:**

1. **Go to the city corporation office** for your ward.
2. **Collect the form** for trade license application.
3. **Fill out the form** with accurate information.
4. **Attach documents** required by the office.
5. **Pay the fee**.
6. **Inspection** may happen at your shop or office.
7. **Collect the license** after approval.

**Online, where available:**

1. Go to the relevant city corporation website.
2. Open the “Trade License” section.
3. Fill out the online form.
4. Upload documents.
5. Pay the fee online.
6. Check application status.
7. Download or collect the license.

## Cost

Trade license fees vary by business type and income:

| Business type | Approximate annual fee |
|---------------|------------------------|
| Small shop with monthly income up to Tk 25,000 | Tk 500-Tk 2,000 |
| Medium shop/office | Tk 2,000-Tk 5,000 |
| Large shop/showroom | Tk 5,000-Tk 15,000 |
| E-commerce/online business | Tk 2,000-Tk 10,000 |
| Restaurant/food shop | Tk 5,000-Tk 20,000 |
| Factory/manufacturing | Tk 10,000-Tk 50,000+ |

> **Note:** Fees may vary by city corporation and ward. Contact the relevant office for exact fees.

## Renewal

A trade license must be renewed **every year**.

| Topic | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Renewal time** | Usually June-July each year |
| **Late fee** | Penalty if not renewed on time, often 50%-100% extra |
| **How** | Apply at the same office or website |
| **Documents needed** | Previous license, TIN, rental agreement, fee |

> **Tip:** Mark the renewal date on your calendar. Penalties increase if you delay.

## Special Cases

### Trade License for Online Businesses

If your business is fully online and has no physical shop:

- You can use your **home address** for the trade license.
- Some city corporations have separate fee structures for “Home-based business” or “E-commerce.”
- If you have a warehouse or office, apply through the city corporation for that location.

### Multiple Branches

Each branch needs a **separate trade license** from the city corporation or municipality of that branch location.

### Food Businesses

If you operate a food business such as a restaurant, catering, or food delivery service, you may also need:

- **Food Safety License** from BSTI / Food Safety Authority
- **Environment clearance** from the Department of Environment for larger operations

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Better approach |
|---------|-----------------|
| Starting without a trade license | Get the license first, then operate |
| Forgetting renewal | Mark the calendar and renew on time |
| Applying in the wrong ward | Apply at the correct ward office for your business location |
| Operating without paying fees | Penalties can be high and the business may be closed |
| Not using a personal address for online business | Use home or office address for online businesses |
