# Trade License: Local Permission to Operate

> **TL;DR:** A trade license records that a business of a stated type operates at a stated address under a local authority. Banks, payment providers, VAT offices and corporate customers often ask for it during document checks. Categories, fees and procedures vary by location, so start with the current rules of your city corporation, municipality or other local authority.

A trade license is not every permit your business may need. Food, medicine, healthcare, factories, import-export, transport and fintech may require separate approvals.

## Who Should Check the Requirement

If you run a shop, office, online store, service business, agency, small production operation, restaurant or training centre, check the trade-license rules for your location. Do not assume that a small or online business is automatically outside the rules.

Customer interviews and free tests are different from regularly taking payments and issuing invoices. Once you start charging, renting a shop or office, or setting up business payments, ask the local authority which category applies and when you should apply.

## Where to Apply

Apply to the authority responsible for the business address.

| Area | Authority to check |
|---|---|
| Dhaka North | [Dhaka North City Corporation](https://dncc.gov.bd) |
| Dhaka South | [Dhaka South City Corporation](https://dscc.gov.bd) |
| Chattogram | [Chattogram City Corporation](https://ccc.gov.bd) |
| Sylhet | [Sylhet City Corporation](https://scc.gov.bd) |
| Rajshahi | [Rajshahi local portal](https://rajshahi.gov.bd) |
| Khulna | [Khulna City Corporation](https://kcc.gov.bd) |
| Other areas | The relevant municipality or local-government office |

Several branches may require separate licenses for their respective addresses. If the business moves, ask whether the license can be updated or a new application is required.

## Documents to Prepare

The list varies by location, category and business type. Commonly requested information includes:

| Document or information | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Application form | The authority's prescribed form |
| National ID | Identity of the owner or applicant |
| Photograph | Applicant identification |
| TIN | Tax identity |
| Tenancy agreement or ownership document | Evidence of the business address |
| Utility bill | Address check |
| Business name and activity | Category selection |
| Company documents | Incorporation certificate, MoA/AoA and board approval where applicable |
| Previous license | Renewal |

Write the business name, address, owner or director name and TIN consistently across documents. A mismatch may require an explanation or correction during later bank, VAT or payment-provider checks.

## Application Process

Some authorities accept online applications; others require an office visit. The usual sequence is to identify the authority, confirm the category, complete the form, submit documents, pay the fee and complete any required inspection.

Category choice matters. Suppose Tanvir sells homemade pickles online from Bogura but writes “general trading” because someone says it is easier. A bank or payment provider may later ask why the licensed activity does not match the real business. He then has an extra correction to make. Check a published category list where available or ask the authority directly. **Write what the business actually does.**

## Fees and Renewal

Fees can depend on the location, category, business size, signboard and local rules. Do not rely on an old blog post or another business's receipt. Ask for the current fee schedule and keep the official receipt.

Trade licenses require renewal under the local authority's rules. Put the renewal date in the calendar and keep scans of the license, receipts and application documents.

## Online Businesses and Regulated Sectors

An online business still needs an address. Some authorities may accept a home address, but confirm the rule before applying. A warehouse, office or stock location may also matter.

A trade license does not replace sector approvals. Food businesses may face food-safety, packaging and labelling rules. Factories may need fire, environmental, labour, utility or boiler approvals. Import-export businesses have separate registration, banking and customs requirements.

## Checklist

- [ ] Identified the authority for the business address
- [ ] Confirmed the correct business category
- [ ] Prepared identity, tax and address documents
- [ ] Prepared company documents where applicable
- [ ] Checked the current fee and renewal rules
- [ ] Kept the license, receipt and application copy
- [ ] Put the renewal date in the calendar
- [ ] Checked for additional sector permits
