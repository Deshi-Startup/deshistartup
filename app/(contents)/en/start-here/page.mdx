# Start Here — A Guide to Building a Startup in Bangladesh

> **In short:** If you are a new founder, your first job is to understand exactly where you stand. This page gives you a step-by-step roadmap from the beginning.

## Why Startups in Bangladesh?

- Bangladesh has a population of around 170 million, and digital adoption is growing quickly.
- Younger consumers and workers are increasingly comfortable with new business models and technology.
- Government programs, ecosystem players, and startup-support platforms are becoming more active.
- Competition is still uneven in many categories, so a focused niche product or service can gain traction quickly.

## Your First Jobs

1. **Choose a problem** — What specific problem do you want to solve?
2. **Understand the customer** — Who experiences this problem?
3. **Understand the Bangladesh context** — How do payments, trust, distribution, and regulation work here?
4. **Validate the idea** — Do not only think about it; test it with real people.
5. **Review the legal and registration roadmap** — Once the idea is validated, prepare for entity setup and licenses.
